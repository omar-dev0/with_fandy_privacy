1. About Fandy

Fandy is a digital platform that connects independent home chefs with customers who enjoy homemade food.
Fandy operates as a marketplace and service facilitator for ordering, payment, and delivery only.

Fandy does not cook, prepare, package, or store food.

Each chef is fully responsible for:

5. Payments & Financial Data

Payments are collected through secure third-party payment gateways.

Fandy does not store credit/debit card details.

Chef earnings are transferred after deducting the platform commission (15%).
